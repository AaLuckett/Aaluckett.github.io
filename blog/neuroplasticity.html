Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

What is Neuroplasticiy?

Neuroplasticity is like your brain's superpower to change and adapt. It means your brain can learn new things, recover from injuries, and get better at stuff you practice. It's like a muscle that gets stronger when you use it, and it works your whole life. So, you can always learn and improve, no matter how old you are!

What is Growth Mindset?

A growth mindset is when you believe you can get better at something if you try and work hard. It's like thinking, "I can learn and improve." Instead of saying, "I can't do it," you say, "I can learn how to do it." It's a positive way of looking at challenges and helps you keep going when things get tough. So, it's all about believing in your potential to grow and get better! This is helping me get through my self doubt at the start of this course.

How does it influence learning?

When you combine neuroplasticity and a growth mindset, you transform into a learning champion. You're aware that your brain can adapt, and you're filled with the confidence and motivation to embrace new challenges and enhance your skills.
